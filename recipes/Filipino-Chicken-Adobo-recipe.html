Filipino Chicken Adobo

Back to Recipes

Being a first-generation Filipino American, I grew up on Filipino foods prepared by my Lola, who helped raised my sisters and I when my mom worked the 12-hour shifts as a nurse. To this day, my favorite comfort food is Chicken Adobo served with white rice. It's always so filling, you can prepare it with other meats instead of chicken, and it's so simple to make.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Cane vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic
  • Peppercorns
  • Bay leaves
